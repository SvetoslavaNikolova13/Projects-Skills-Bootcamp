We love to spend free time in our garden, no matter the season. ...and that is where idea came to make our lovely delicious jams. We start planting seeds in springtime and already see their first results come summer. The best thing about this time is the bags full of homemade jam jars when our guests leave. We have long since given up on store bought jam, and after all, how can you beat something that will always taste of home? You can see selection of our jams below.
